What is Fit to Print?

This project was completed in three weeks as a part of the Insight Data Science program.

Fit to print is a tool that helps you match the writing style of your article to the editorial voice of a desired publication.

It has learned the stylistic markers of articles from 9 major publications with a variety of approaches to reporting news and compares your article to others that appeared in these publications.
