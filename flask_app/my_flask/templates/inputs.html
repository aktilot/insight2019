Can I say that?

Choose your audience:

Do you worry about the tone of your writing?

'Can I say that?' is a simple tool that gives you a sense for how others may perceive your writing. It was developed over a few weeks as a pilot project for the Insight Data Science program.

It's digested thousands of pages from forums, blog posts, government reports, and PhD dissertations, to learn how we express ourselves in different contexts.

Try it out! Enter that email you've been staring at, and we'll let you know if it's better on Reddit or in your boss' inbox.
